 About

Yasas Seneviratne received the honors degree in electronic and telecommunications engineering from University of Moratuwa, Sri Lanka, in 2017. He is currently a Ph.D. candidate in the department of computer engineering at the University of Virginia, USA, co-advised by Prof. Kevin Skadron and Prof. Samira Khan . His research focuses on computer systems, accelerators, architectures for runtime monitoring of hardware ( for verification, correctness, and security), and real system prototyping. Before starting his Ph.D., he worked at Synopsys as a Research and Development Engineer.
